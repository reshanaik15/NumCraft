Division
Division is the process of distributing a number into equal parts, or finding how many times one number is contained within another:
Definition: Division is the operation of splitting a number into equal parts or determining how many times one number can be subtracted from another.
Symbol: The symbol for division is the division sign, (รท), or a forward slash (/).
Result: The result of division is called the quotient. Sometimes, division results in a remainder.
Explanation: Division can be thought of as "sharing" or "grouping" equally. For example, dividing 12 by 3 means splitting 12 into 3 equal parts, resulting in 4.
